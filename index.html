About Us

Blooms by Elle started on June 26, 2025, when Liezel De Tomas discovered her love for making satin flower bouquets. Inspired by her cousin, she turned that passion into a small business built with care and creativity. Every bouquet is handmade with love using quality materials to make sure each one brings joy and smiles. Based in Old Balara, Quezon City, Blooms by Elle aims to make someone happy with every bloom affordable, beautiful, and made from the heart.
